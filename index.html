About Us

“We ought to be about something beyond moving chicken. We ought to be a piece of our clients’ lives and the networks in which we serve.”

Our originator, settled on the choice to close on Sundays in 2016 when he opened his first eatery in New Town, Kolkata. Having worked seven days seven days in eateries open 24 hours, Truett saw the significance of shutting on Sundays with the goal that he and his representatives could set aside one day to rest and love whether they pick – a training we maintain today.
